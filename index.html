FAQ

How does Revaia learn my brand voice?
During onboarding (48 hours), we analyze your existing responses, your website copy, and conduct a 20-minute call to understand your tone, values, and what your property stands for. The AI is then calibrated to your voice — not a generic hotel template.
What if a review requires a very specific response?
For reviews under 3 stars, you receive an instant SMS/WhatsApp alert before the response is sent. You have a 30-minute window to review or modify. For all other reviews, responses are published automatically.
Which platforms do you cover?
Google, Booking.com, TripAdvisor, Expedia, Hotels.com, and Airbnb. We're adding new platforms continuously. If you need a specific platform covered, we'll prioritize it for your account.
Can I cancel anytime?
Monthly plans cancel anytime with no penalty. Annual plans include a 30-day satisfaction guarantee — if you're not satisfied in the first 30 days, full refund. No questions asked.
How is this different from MARA or TrustYou?
MARA is a tool — your team still generates and sends each response (about 2 minutes per review). TrustYou is built for hotel chains with IT teams. Revaia is a full service — your team touches nothing, ever. Set it up once and forget it exists.
What does setup look like?
You complete a 20-minute onboarding form. We handle the rest. Platform access, brand voice calibration, system testing — all within 48 hours. Your first automated response goes live before the end of the week.
