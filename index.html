FAQ

Every question
answered.

Will the responses sound robotic or generic?
No. During onboarding, we calibrate REVAIA to your property's exact voice — your tone, your values, your vocabulary. Responses reference specific details from each review. GMs who've seen the output consistently say it reads better than what they would have written themselves.
What happens with a VIP guest or a sensitive complaint?
For any review under 3 stars, REVAIA sends you an instant SMS/WhatsApp alert before publishing. You have a 30-minute window to review, edit, or override. For 4 and 5-star reviews — responses go live automatically. You're always in control where it matters most.
How long does setup take?
48 hours. You complete a 20-minute onboarding form. We handle platform access, brand voice calibration, and system testing. Your first automated response goes live before the end of the week.
Which platforms are covered?
Google, Booking.com, TripAdvisor, Expedia, Hotels.com, and Airbnb. All scanned every 30 minutes, 24/7. If you need a specific platform prioritized, we handle it during onboarding.
How is this different from MARA?
MARA saves you time — your team still reads every review and approves every response. That's still 30–60 minutes a week. REVAIA is full autopilot: nothing reaches your team's inbox unless it's a 1 or 2-star alert. Set it up once. Forget it exists.
Can I cancel anytime?
Monthly plans cancel anytime, no questions, no penalty. Annual plans include a 30-day full refund guarantee. If REVAIA doesn't deliver in the first 30 days, you get every dollar back.
